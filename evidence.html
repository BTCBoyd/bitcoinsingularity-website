About This Tracker

The single most comprehensive resource tracking the convergence of AI agents and Bitcoin. Every announcement, launch, integration, and data point—timestamped, categorized, and honestly evaluated.

We track supporting evidence (Lightning integrations, L402 deployments, agent wallet launches) and competing evidence (x402/USDC adoption, traditional payment rail integrations). Intellectual honesty is the brand.

Data Methodology

Source Verification: All data points include source links to official announcements, GitHub repos, blog posts, or academic publications. No hearsay.

Timestamp Accuracy: Dates reflect official announcement/launch, not when we discovered it. Updates include revision timestamps.

Competitive Honesty: We don't hide data that challenges the Bitcoin-centric thesis. If x402/USDC is outpacing L402/Bitcoin, we document that and analyze why.
